OptoPrimDB

OptoPrimDB is an early-stage, experimental database that aims to collect and organize published optogenetics studies in non-human primates. The goal is to provide a simple, searchable overview of the primate optogenetics literature with standardized paper-level metadata.

This is a very early testing version. The database is incomplete, entries may change, and the structure is still evolving.
